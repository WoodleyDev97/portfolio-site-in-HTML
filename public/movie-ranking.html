The Best Movies According to Nick Woodley

My Top 3 Favourite Movies of all time

Harry Potter series

A classic saga with some of the best story telling ever told in a magical setting

Shawshank Redemption

An excellent story about a man being sent to prison and his life on the inside

Lord of the Rings

A fantasy classic about a band of warriors and an uncoordinated hobbit who need to go to the most daring place in existance to destroy an evil ring who corrupts anyone who wears it while revealing their current location
